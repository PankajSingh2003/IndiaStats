DRDO

DRDO stands for Defence Research and Development Organization, which is an organization under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. It is responsible for the development of technology for the Indian armed forces, with various research centers located across the country specializing in different areas of research.

Live Link

Some Known Drdo Centres in India

    Joint Advanced Technology Centre (JATC) at IIT Delhi
    Jagadish Chandra Bose Centre of Advanced Technology (JCBCAT) at Jadavpur University Kolkata
    Bi-nodal (IIT B and IIT M) Centre of Propulsion Technology (CoPT) at IIT Bombay
    Research & Innovation Centre (RIC) at IIT Madras Research Park, Chennai
    DRDO-Bharthiar University Centre for Life Sciences (DRDO-BU CLS) Coimbatore
    Advanced Centre of Research in High Energy Materials (ACRHEM), University of Hyderabad
    East Science and Technology Centre (NESTC) at Mizoram University, Aizawl
    Kalam Centre for Science and Technology (KCST) at Central University of Jammu (CUJ)
